# How to run Jarvis — your daily rhythm

> Plain-English. No jargon. If you only read one doc, read this. The whole point: **sit down, know the
> ONE thing, do it.** Everything else is noise the system handles for you.

---

## The 60-second morning

1. **Open Jarvis.** You land on **Home**.
2. Read the **🎯 one thing** at the top. That is your single highest-leverage move right now — already
   chosen for you (almost always: a gov proposal that needs your signature, because gov is priority #1).
   **Do that first.** Don't scroll past it.
3. Glance at the **ticker** up top (the rolling "⏳ N awaiting you"). That's the gated 5% — things the
   system prepared but will not do without your yes. Tap it to review.
4. Glance at **today's tasks** right below. Tap the circle to check one off.

That's the morning. If you do nothing else, do the one thing.

---

## The four places (that's all there is)

| Tab | What it's for | What you do there |
|---|---|---|
| **Home** | The glance. | See the one thing, the ticker, today's tasks. Start here. |
| **Today** | Your to-dos + calendar. | Add/complete tasks, see your week, dump a thought into Capture. |
| **Talk** | Ask Jarvis anything. | "What's the news on X", "draft this", "what's my next gov move". |
| **Gov Pipeline** (More → Gov Pipeline) | Your business, on one board. | See where every contract stands + your next move. |

Everything under **More → "Behind the scenes"** (Floor, HQ, Command, Dashboard) is optional eye-candy.
Ignore it. It does not run your business; **you + the four places above do.**

---

## Your business in one board (Gov Pipeline)

The straight line, every time:

> **Find → Score → Respond → Build past performance → Track**

Jarvis scouts SAM.gov daily and drops everything in your lane onto the board:
**janitorial · custodial · grounds · under $150k · Small-Business / SDB set-asides** (it flags and sets
aside anything you can't prime — 8(a), SDVOSB, WOSB, HUBZone).

The board has five columns — left to right is how a deal moves:

- **Found** — Jarvis found + scored it. It's just watching. *Nothing for you to do.*
- **Reviewing** — worth a look. **Your move:** say "pursue" and Jarvis drafts the response.
- **Responding** — Jarvis drafted it. **Your move:** review, sign & submit.
- **Submitted** — it's in. Jarvis waits for the agency.
- **Won / Lost** — you mark the outcome (Won / Lost / Pass on any card).

**The key thing on every card: who's next.** 👤 **You** or 🤖 **Jarvis**. If it says 🤖 Jarvis, relax —
it's handled. If it says 👤 You, that's your to-do. The **YOUR NEXT MOVE** banner at the top is always
the single most important one.

---

## What's automatic vs. what needs you

**Jarvis does on its own:** scouting SAM.gov, scoring opportunities, drafting proposals and outreach,
sorting email, finding subcontractors, tracking everything.

**Only you can do (the gated 5%):** sign & submit a proposal, send an email, spend money. Jarvis prepares
the whole thing and waits. You approve with one tap (in the ticker, on Home, or in the Gov board). Nothing
irreversible happens without you — by design.

So "running your business" is really just: **do the one thing, and clear the approvals when they pop up.**
That's it.

---

## Weekly (10 minutes)

- Open the **Gov Pipeline**. Anything in **Reviewing** with 4–5 stars you actually want? Say "pursue."
- Anything you're not chasing? Hit **Pass** so the board stays clean.
- Check **Won / Lost** is honest.

---

## When you're stuck

Just ask in **Talk**: *"What should I be doing right now?"* or *"What's my next gov move?"* — it answers
from the same board, so it'll never contradict what you see. You can run the whole thing by asking.
